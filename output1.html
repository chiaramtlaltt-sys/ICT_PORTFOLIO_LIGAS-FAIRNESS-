Rainbow rises

Potent sunrises,

Catch my breath, so much so,

spin around,

Rainbow sunrises,

It's like God is smiling

and saying: "you ain't seen nothing yet!" -

After the gray and overcast days, followed by rain,

followed by drizzle followed by deluge;

the devil's cry,

God's refuge.
